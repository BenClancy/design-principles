KPIs

Data you need to measure and monitor

The digital by default service standard sets four Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to measure the success of your service: cost per transaction, user satisfaction, completion rate and digital take-up.

For each KPI you will work with GDS to set a sensible goal and interim milestones on the way towards meeting them.

Once live, service performance will be monitored and displayed on the performance platform.

The KPIs

Understand more about how to define, measure and implement the KPIs:

  • cost per transaction
  • user satisfaction
  • completion rate
  • digital take-up
